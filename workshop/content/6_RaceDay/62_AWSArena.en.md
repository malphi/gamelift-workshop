## Switch to the shared arena

Time to race real humans. Back on the login screen:

1. Click **switch racer** if you're in a lobby, then choose **☁️ AWS ARENA**
2. Log in (same name works — arenas have separate player databases, so you'll
   get a fresh persona here)

Now every participant is in **one shared world**: the world chat on the right
shows everyone joining, and the leaderboard is global.

## Tournament time 🏁

Your instructor will organize groups. Suggested format:

- **Round 1**: pairs — both players pick the same track, queue **2P**
- **Round 2**: threes — queue **3P** (FlexMatch groups by track, so agree on
  the track first!)
- **Finals**: top finishers queue **4P** on the hardest unlocked track

Racing tips:

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| ← / → (or A / D) | change lane — one tap, one lane |
| Space | use held item: 🔥 nitro (speed boost) or 💣 bomb (drops behind you) |

Watch the world chat between races — match announcements show who's racing whom,
and the cyan debug lines show FlexMatch working in real time.

## Checkpoint ★

You've completed at least one multiplayer race on AWS ARENA against another
participant. Check the **LEADERBOARD** — is your best time on the board?
