*Duration: ~20 minutes*

In Module 4 anyone who picked a track shared a session with the next person —
no rules. Now add the missing intelligence: **who** should share a race. Rule
sets, tickets, and the event pipeline — then you'll turn on FlexMatch in front
of the *same* EC2 fleet and watch it enforce level balancing, same-track, team
sizes and latency-based placement.
